Joshua Tree

We started out the new year right by taking a drive to Joshua Tree National Park. There, we hiked until the sun went down. We saw cacti, succulents, yucca trees, and jack rabbits. There was so much beautiful creation to take in. We ended the evening with pizza, pasta, and a hot tub! The following morning, we got up and took another hike to 49 palms oasis. In the middle of no where pops out a bunch of palm trees and running water. It was very cool to see with my own eyes. After that, we drove back to L.A. and enjoyed some much-needed relaxation after all of the hiking we did.
