Fire On The Beach

Once we fully recovered from the exhausting hike, a plan needed to be made for what to do next. Our daylight window was getting smaller and smaller, so time was of the essence. In order to enjoy the rest of the daylight and transition nicely into the evening, we decided to grab some food, wine, and fire wood. Once the items were procured, we found a beach that had first come- first serve fire pits. Fortunately, we arrive just before a huge wave of people came onto the beach and took up the rest of the fire pits. We took pictures of the beautiful sun set and tried to get some good photos of us siblings. The California wine was tasty, the firewood was aromatic, and the bites of food were all we needed for everything to be perfect.
